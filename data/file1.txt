India is a country in South Asia. It is the seventh-largest country by land area and the second-most populous country in the world. 
The capital of India is New Delhi, and its largest city is Mumbai. India has 28 states and 8 union territories. 
The official languages are Hindi and English. India is known for its diverse culture, festivals, and rich history.
Famous landmarks include the Taj Mahal, Red Fort, and Qutub Minar.
